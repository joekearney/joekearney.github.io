What's this all about?

We'd like to create a memento of our wedding that we'll be able to use everyday. And we like food, and we'd really like to get better at cooking it.

So our plan is to create a guest book from all your tasty recipes that everyone can then sign on the day. Then we'll be reminded of all of you and our wedding day every time we cook a tasty dinner.
