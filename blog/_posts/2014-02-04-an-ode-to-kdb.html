(with apologies to William Walton)

Kx was a great city
Her merchandise was of column-per-file,
Of precious types, of errors, of fine syntax
Of speed, ease and simplicity
All manner vessels of idiocy
All manner vessels of bad design choices
Of sv, :: and value,
Integers, floats and matrices,
Of efficiency, wine and mmu,
Fine -15!, tilde, max
Abort, scan, iterate, quit
And the souls of men.
